# Sales AI Agent — Frequently Asked Questions

> Created: March 2, 2026 | Updated: March 2, 2026

## Process & Getting Started

**How does the process work?**
It starts with a discovery call where we understand your sales process, qualification criteria, and tech stack. From there we design the conversation flows, build the knowledge base, configure the agent, integrate it with your tools, test it, and deploy it on your website.

**How long does it take to launch?**
It depends on the complexity of your sales process and number of integrations. A typical project takes several weeks from discovery to launch. Exact timelines are discussed on the discovery call.

**What do you need from us to get started?**
We'll need access to your sales process documentation (or a walkthrough), information about your ideal customer profile, common questions your leads ask, any existing sales materials (pitch decks, case studies), and access to the tools we'll integrate with (CRM, calendar, website).

**Do we need to build or configure anything ourselves?**
No. Halo Lab handles the full build end-to-end — from conversation design to deployment. Your team participates in discovery and reviews the agent during testing, but the technical work is on us.

## Integrations & Tech

**What tools does it integrate with?**
The agent can integrate with CRMs (e.g., HubSpot), calendar tools (e.g., Calendly), website platforms, Slack for notifications, and other tools depending on your stack. Specific integrations are scoped during discovery.

**Will it work on our website?**
Yes. The agent is deployed as a chat widget that can be embedded on any website — regardless of the platform (WordPress, Webflow, custom-built, etc.).

**Does it work on mobile?**
Yes. The chat widget is fully responsive and works on desktop and mobile browsers.

**Can it connect to our CRM?**
Yes. The agent integrates with popular CRMs like HubSpot, Salesforce, and others. Lead data from conversations (contact info, qualification score, conversation summary) can flow into your CRM. Specific CRM integration is scoped during the discovery call.

## Capabilities & Scope

**What can the agent actually do?**
It qualifies inbound leads through natural conversation, answers common questions about your services from a curated knowledge base, books meetings with your sales team, and hands off qualified leads with a pre-call brief so your team is prepared.

**Can it handle complex or unusual questions?**
The agent answers questions based on the knowledge base we build for it. If a question falls outside the KB, the agent will honestly say it doesn't have the answer and offer to connect the visitor with your team via email or a call.

**Will it replace our sales team?**
No. The agent handles the top of the funnel — initial conversations, qualification, and meeting booking. It augments your sales team by filtering leads and preparing them for calls. Your sales managers still handle discovery calls and closing.

**Does it work 24/7?**
Yes. The agent is available around the clock, including nights, weekends, and holidays. Visitors can have a full conversation and book a meeting at any time.

## After Launch

**What happens after launch?**
Once live, we monitor the agent's conversations, review performance, and tune the prompts and knowledge base based on real interactions. This ongoing optimization is available through an optional monthly retainer.

**Can we update the knowledge base ourselves?**
The knowledge base can be updated at any time. Your team can manage updates independently, and Halo Lab is always happy to help with ongoing maintenance and optimization.

**How do we know if it's working?**
The platform provides analytics: number of conversations, qualification rates, booking rates, and conversation logs. You can review every conversation the agent has. Performance is also reviewed during optimization cycles.
