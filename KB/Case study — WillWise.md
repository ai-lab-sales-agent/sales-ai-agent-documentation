# Case Study — WillWise

> Created: March 2, 2026 | Updated: March 2, 2026

## Overview

- **Product:** WillWise — AI-powered assistant for Slack
- **Type:** Halo Lab internal product
- **Status:** Live — available as a Slack app
- **Website:** https://willwise.ai/

## Challenge

Teams lose time scrolling through long Slack threads to find key decisions, context, or action items. Important information gets buried, and writing announcements or answering repeated questions pulls people away from focused work.

## Solution

Halo Lab built and released WillWise — an AI-powered Slack app that helps teams and individuals work smarter without leaving Slack. The app:

- Summarises conversations so team members can catch up quickly
- Drafts announcements based on context
- Answers questions using workspace knowledge
- Keeps everyone focused, aligned, and productive

## What This Demonstrates

- Halo Lab builds and ships production-ready AI products end-to-end
- Experience with conversational AI that understands context and generates natural responses
- Ability to integrate AI into existing tools (Slack) that teams already use
- Full product lifecycle: concept → build → release → live users
