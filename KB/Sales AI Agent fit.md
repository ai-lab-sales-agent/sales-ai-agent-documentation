# Sales AI Agent — Who It's For & Expected Outcomes

> Created: March 2, 2026 | Updated: March 2, 2026

## Who It's For

The Sales AI Agent is designed for B2B companies that:
- Receive inbound leads through their website
- Have a sales team that handles discovery calls
- Want to respond to leads faster (seconds instead of hours)
- Need to filter out unqualified leads before they reach a sales manager
- Want 24/7 coverage without hiring additional SDRs

### Typical Industries
SaaS, Fintech, Edtech, Healthcare, Real Estate, Logistics, Marketplace, ML/AI companies — and any B2B business with an inbound sales motion.

## Key Outcomes

- **Instant response** — Every lead gets a response in under 3 seconds, 24/7
- **Higher qualification rate** — Only qualified leads reach the sales team
- **More booked meetings** — Frictionless booking directly from the chat
- **Better-prepared calls** — Sales managers receive a pre-call brief with full context
- **Fewer wasted calls** — Unqualified leads are filtered out before they consume sales time
- **Consistent experience** — Every visitor gets the same professional, consultative interaction

## Limitations the Agent Should Communicate Honestly

- Exact pricing depends on the scope and is discussed on a discovery call
- Timelines depend on the complexity of the sales process and integrations needed
- ROI depends on the client's traffic volume, current conversion rates, and sales cycle
- The agent works best with a steady flow of inbound website traffic
