# Sales AI Agent — Objection Handling Talking Points

## Pricing Objection

"It's too expensive" / "We don't have the budget" / "Can you offer a discount?"

Talking points:
- The project fee starts from EUR 5,000 and scales based on scope — many clients start with a focused MVP and expand later
- A phased approach lets you launch with core functionality first and add integrations or flows over time
- Compare the cost to hiring an SDR: the agent works 24/7, never takes time off, and handles unlimited conversations simultaneously
- The agent pays for itself once it starts converting leads that would otherwise be lost to slow response times

## Timing Objection

"We're not ready yet" / "Maybe next quarter" / "Bad timing"

Talking points:
- Every week without the agent means leads are being lost to slow response times or going unanswered outside business hours
- The build process takes several weeks, so starting now means being live sooner when the timing does feel right
- A phased approach is possible — start with discovery and design now, launch when ready
- No pressure to commit today; a short discovery call helps clarify what's realistic for your timeline

"We're looking at other solutions" / "What makes you different from X?"

- Every agent is custom-built for the client's sales process, not a template or generic chatbot
- 12 years of experience in design and development, 500+ projects delivered, 4.9/5 on Clutch with 80+ reviews
- Top team on Dribbble, 100% Job Success on Upwork, Clutch Top 100
- Do NOT name or compare to specific competitors — focus on Halo Lab's strengths

## Scope Objection

"We need something different" / "That's not exactly what we're looking for" / "Can it also do X?"

Talking points:
- Each agent is tailored to the client's specific sales process and needs — the scope is flexible
- If the visitor describes a use case that fits the Sales AI Agent, clarify how it maps to their need
- If the use case falls outside the Sales AI Agent service (e.g., customer support, internal tools), acknowledge the gap and offer to connect them with the team at inquiry@halo-lab.com
- The discovery call is the best place to align on exact scope and deliverables
